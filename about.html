Bytras is an eco-conscious company dedicated to creating innovative, sustainable solutions that drive human efficiency while prioritizing environmental responsibility.

01.

Born from recycled plastics bags collected by our non-profit organisation, each peice tells a tale of possibility. This isn't just any material - it's a testament to show that we can protect our planet - one up-cycled plastic at a time.

02.

The recycled plastic we use go through an intensive cleaning process, thoroughly washing and removing any remnants from their past. Next, through shredding and heat-pressing methods, 2,plastics are transformed into hard, durable sheets.

03.

Harnessing the precision of High-end technology, the sheets are intricately sculpted . Designed to optimise both and style and strength, every sheets is thoughtfully crafted through innovation to be as sustainable & Durable as possible.
